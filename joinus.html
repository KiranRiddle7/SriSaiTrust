The Trust aims at the service of the deserved people from all walks of life. It is run with the concept that we are all instruments in His hands. In fact, nobody serves & nobody is served, it is all the Supreme Soul who plays the dual role. So, we heartily welcome the people into our Trust those we are interested to take part in the service oriented activities without selfish motives.



As a Volunteer

Those who can not offer their help in the form of money they can render their services in their capacity.

Get on with your worldly activities cheerfully, but do not forget God. Do not kick against the pricks of life.

Whatever creature comes to you, human or otherwise, treat it with consideration.


As A Member

If you decided to join us and play a vital role then you are supposed to register yourself as a member of the Trust.

First give food to the hungry and then eat yourself. Never eat anything without giving something from it to others. Giving the food to hungry people or animals is nothing but giving it to god.

Shirdi Sai Baba
As per all religious holy scriptures, one should spend 10% to 15% of their income for serving the needy. So the Members of the trust are supposed to donate 10% to 15% of their income of the every month and should Trust in the Sathguru Sai fully and chant his name which is called “SADHANA”. Then as said in “sri sai sath charitraamrutham”.

If a devotee is about to fall, I stretch out my hands to support him or her.

Shirdi Sai Baba

  • Free medical facilities for their family as per suitable system of medicinal system.
  • Suggestions & Solutions to various problems they come across in their life.
  • Students will get career counselling.
  • Free preventive medicine is supplied from S S Multitherapy Clinic for all seasonal diseases.
  • Free Stress Management Counselling for all walks of life thus get rid of various diseases.
