Why SST?

Even after independence we still find so many inequalities in our society. Many people came forward and many are still making incessant efforts to eradicate them in all possible ways. Even our government is implementing various schemes for the upliftment of economically and socially backward people.

A number of charitable trusts, foundations, NGO's, Christian Missionaries etc. have been established to give their helping hand for the service of helpless people like orphans, mentally & physically challenged people and also aged people. Some of the above organizations are providing quality education for poor students at free of cost. Some other organizations are offering quality medical aid for the poor at free of cost. In this way a lot of people are doing their best for the wellbeing of the entire mankind without any selfish motives.

Sri Sai Trust was established in the year 2015 with the intention of making an effort to establish a new society where people think that “As all living beings are nothing but incarnation of the Almighty so every one must follow the principle that “Humanity is true religion” and should not have any inequalities among them.

and with the motto of
stretching a helping hand for the service of the helpless, the destitute, the shelterless and those who are deserved....

As Saibaba encouraged charity and said, "Unless there is some relationship or connection, nobody goes anywhere. If any men or creatures come to you, do not discourteously drive them away, but receive them well and treat them with due respect."

Who We Are?

Founder Trustee
It is solely an intensive motivation of Bhagavan Sri Samardha Sadguru Sainath Maharaj that has lead to the establishment of the Trust. It was thought long before as I got a call from Guru. But, I couldn't put my fullest effort towards it since I had been busy with my personal & professional commitments. Later, my health started degenerating so fast that I underwent a surgery resulting in removal of one of my lungs. I had to face the toughest time as it suddenly turned against me. Moreover, my financial crisis added fuel to the fire, throwing my family into a helpless state. But, I continued my steadfast and invincible faith in my Guru despite the unfavorable circumstances. Later, my health has been stabilized to some extent by His grace and to my surprise, things started turning so favourable that the Trust was established within a short period of time.

Objectives

The trust shall be an irrevocable public educational, cultural trust for the benefit of all chosen and deserved persons belonging to whatever community irrespective of Caste, Creed or Religion.

The objectives for which the Trust is founded are:

  1. To establish, maintain and run Schools, Colleges, Yoga and Meditations Centres, Social Service Centres, Industrial Training Centres and Vocational Training Centres.
  2. To advance Indian Culture and Literature, Service of this Country for the benefit of the Nation.
  3. To train teachers and workers in ideal and practice of the true spirit of the education and learning.
  4. To establish research and training centres for the furtherance of education/learning in it’s various fields and branches.
  5. To Provide medical aid in all systems and methods prevailing in the country of operation to needy irrespective of caste, creed, color and sex.
  6. To promote literacy among all and diffuse useful knowledge to all to enable them to lead a happy and harmonious life and maintain peace in the area.
  7. To establish and maintain Rescue Homes for destitute, old age homes and orphanage homes at such places as decided by the Board of Trustees.
  8. To provide legal aid,family counseling,counseling against suicide and drug de-addiction counseling.

Our Patron

Sai Baba of Shirdi, also known as Shirdi Sai Baba, was an Indian spiritual master who was regarded by his devotees as a saint, fakir, and satguru, according to their individual proclivities and beliefs.

What is new in the world? Nothing. What is old in the world? Nothing. Everything has always been and will always be..

Shirdi Sai Baba
He taught a moral code of love, forgiveness, helping others, charity, contentment, inner peace, and devotion to God and guru.

One of his well known epigrams, “Sabka Malik Ek” (“One God governs all”), is associated with Hinduism, Islam and Sufism. He also said, “Trust in me and your prayer shall be answered”. He always uttered “Allah Malik” (“God is King”).

Sai baba of shirdi reported many miracles. He performed many miracles such as levitation, bilocation, materialization, reading of peoples mind, entering tomb at his own will,removal of body parts and affixing them again including intestines. Took rebirth after 3 days, cured the ill, and saved the people by stopping the falling mosque. There are hundreds of miracles reported at shirdi by shirdi sai baba. People believe that reading sai charitra is good for all types of problems in the family and all health issues. There are several blogs written on sai baba and his day to day miracles in devotee life. This is increasing lots of faith in people. Devotee often says that. Shirdi sai baba gave darshan to them in form of lord rama, Krishna etc., Many stories were framed on shirdi of sai baba by his followers. They say that saibaba used to come in dream and gave them advice on what to do and what not to do.
